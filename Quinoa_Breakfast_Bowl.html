Quinoa Breakfast Bowl

Yield: 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup diced apples
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon walnuts or almonds, chopped

Instructions

  1. Warm quinoa with almond milk in a small saucepan over low heat.
  2. Stir in cinnamon.
  3. Top with diced apples and nuts. Serve warm.
