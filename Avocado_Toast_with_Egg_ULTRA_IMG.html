Avocado Toast with Egg

Yield: 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 1 slice whole grain bread
  • 1/2 ripe avocado
  • 1 egg, poached or boiled
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • Pinch of black pepper

Instructions

  1. Toast bread.
  2. Mash avocado with lemon juice.
  3. Spread on toast.
  4. Top with egg and black pepper.
