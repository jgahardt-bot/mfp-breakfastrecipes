Overnight Oats with Chia and Berries

Yield: 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Instructions

  1. Combine oats, chia, and cinnamon.
  2. Stir in almond milk, yogurt, vanilla, and honey (optional).
  3. Fold in berries.
  4. Cover and refrigerate at least 6 hours.
  5. Stir and add a splash of milk before serving.
