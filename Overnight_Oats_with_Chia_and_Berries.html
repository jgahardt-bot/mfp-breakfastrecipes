Overnight Oats with Chia & Berries

Yield: 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon honey (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)

Instructions

  1. Combine oats, chia, cinnamon in a jar.
  2. Stir in almond milk, yogurt, vanilla, and honey (if using).
  3. Fold in berries.
  4. Cover and refrigerate at least 6 hours (overnight best).
  5. Stir and add a splash of milk before serving, if needed.
