Spinach Berry Smoothie

Yield: 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 cup fresh spinach leaves (lightly packed)
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries (blueberries, raspberries, strawberries)
  • 1/2 banana
  • 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon ground flaxseed
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon (optional)
  • 4–5 ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Add almond milk to blender.
  2. Add spinach, berries, banana, and Greek yogurt.
  3. Add flaxseed, cinnamon, and ice.
  4. Blend until completely smooth (30–60 seconds).
  5. Adjust thickness with a splash of water or more ice, to taste.
